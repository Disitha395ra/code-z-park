About Code-Z-Park 2024

The annual coding extravaganza hosted by the University of Vavuniya! Prepare to be immersed in a digital adventure where coding prowess takes center stage. As the heartbeat of innovation, Code-Z-Park brings together students, coding enthusiasts, and industry professionals to showcase their talents and tackle coding challenges that push boundaries.

What we plan to do

Get ready for the Code-Z-Park event at the University of Vavuniya, featuring two thrilling rounds of coding challenges. The Preliminary Coding Competition kicks off the day, offering participants two hours to showcase their coding skills on Hackerrank in their preferred language. Following this, the Web Development Competition takes center stage, a four-hour team-based challenge where participants collaboratively design and build innovative web applications. At the end of the event, we'll award valuable certificates to the top five performers, while all participants will receive recognition with participation certificates. Join us for a day of coding excitement and creative web development at Code-Z-Park!

EventGuidelines

Adhere to designated time frames for both competitions, and bring your own laptops with the necessary equipment. Display good sportsmanship and respect for fellow competitors, avoiding any disruptive or inappropriate behavior. The decisions of the judges are final, and any concerns should be communicated to the event organizers in a respectful manner.
